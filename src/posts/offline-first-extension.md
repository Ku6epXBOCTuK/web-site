# offline-first chrome extension: lessons from building XBOCT-page

why i switched from start.me to building my own extension.

## The Problem

start.me was slow. Every time i opened a new tab, i had to wait for it to load.

## The Solution

Build my own extension with offline-first architecture.

- Instant load from local storage
- Sync in background
- No subscriptions, no ads
